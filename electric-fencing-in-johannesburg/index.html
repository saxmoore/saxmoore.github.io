What you didn’t know about Electric Fencing

Has your home ever been broken into? Were you at home when it happened? Or do you know of someone who’s house got broken into and all their valuable possessions were taken? Knowing that strangers can enter your property is a nerve-wracking thought! It raises a fear within you that cannot be silenc+ed.

If this is the case, your only option is to upgrade and improve your security with Electric Fencing in Johannesburg.

You might not think that you need some added security around your property, but even the tallest brick walls have proven to be no match for intruders. By installing Electric Fencing in Johannesburg, you can increase your security by 90% and deter even the most determined intruders.

Here are a few examples why added security will be more than beneficial to you.

Benefits of Electric Fencing in Johannesburg:

1. Added Security

Even if you already have an existing wall or palisades surrounding your property, you can add an Electric fence and not only heighten the security but add physical height to your perimeter, improving the quality of your wall or fence altogether. Saxmoore can be the reason you get a decent night’s sleep without worrying about the dangers out there.

2. Aesthetic Value

Electric Fencing in Johannesburg is ideal for properties that would benefit from a fence that allows the surrounding areas to be visible from inside the property; if you live on a farmland or on a property outside of town and you don’t want a brick wall, but you still require protection that would allow you to keep the view of your extended backyard, then Electric fencing is your answer. It will simultaneously give you the security you need and the visibility you want.

Another benefit of Electric fencing in Johannesburg is the visibility it offers companies in industrial areas; instead of concealing your business behind tall brick walls, you could be visible to the public by installing Electric fencing abound your buildings, keeping your assets and employees safe, as well as being practical for business purposes.

3. Upgrade to your Property

One of the most effective ways of adding value to your property is by adding security features. Clear view fencing in Johannesburg is a highly effective way of upgrading your property and increasing your resale value, should you plan on selling it one day. Saxmoore also has CCTV cameras and motorized gates which they supply and install.

4. Protecting what’s Yours

The obvious benefits of installing Electric fencing in Johannesburg are the security features it offers. With the crime rates being what they are in Gauteng, anybody is better off with electric fencing around their house or office. You want to be safe from burglars and intruders alike, why not invest in a quality electric fence.

5. Save on Maintenance

The materials used for Electric fencing are durable and low maintenance, meaning that you won’t need to replace any of the materials if you take good care of your fence. Shrubbery has to be trimmed back in order to keep the fence clear and the voltages have to be maintained at a certain level, but other than that, your clear view fence won’t cost you nearly as much in maintenance as other kinds of perimeters.

6. Keep pets Save

Not only will Saxmoore’s fences keep unwanted visitors out, it is highly effective in keeping pets or livestock inside your property as well. If you have dogs who you love and is also a part of your family, you might consider keeping them safe with electric fencing in Johannesburg. Some criminals have gone as far as poisoning dogs to gain access to properties – prevent this from happening by installing an effective barrier around your property to keep your pets safe.

There are many other benefits of Electric Fencing in Johannesburg, like the low costs involved with the installation which is also easier than traditional barriers. Saxmoore installs certified fences that guarantee their compliance with the Occupational Health and Safety Act 85, so you can rest assured that your property is properly protected against intruders.

Don’t think that it will never happen to you because crime does not discriminate – we all fall victim to it at some point, but you can prevent being part of the statistics of crime in Johannesburg. Contact Saxmoore today for a quote and make your home as safe as can be.
